1. Squats

Muscles Targeted: Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Glutes

Steps:

  1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Bend your knees and lower your hips as if sitting back into a chair.
  3. Lower down until your thighs are parallel to the ground.
  4. Push through your heels to return to the starting position.

Form Tips:

  • Keep your back straight and chest up.
  • Avoid letting your knees go beyond your toes.

2. Lunges

Muscles Targeted: Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Glutes

Steps:

  1. Take a step forward with one leg, lowering your hips until both knees are bent at a 90-degree angle.
  2. Push off the front foot to return to the starting position.
  3. Repeat on the other leg.

Form Tips:

  • Keep your upper body straight.
  • Avoid leaning too far forward.

3. Leg Press

Muscles Targeted: Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Glutes

Steps:

  1. Sit at the leg press machine with your back against the pad.
  2. Push the platform away by extending your knees.
  3. Lower the platform back towards you.

Form Tips:

  • Adjust the machine to align your knees with your hips.
  • Keep your feet flat on the platform.

4. Calf Raises

Muscles Targeted: Calves

Steps:

  1. Stand on a flat surface with feet hip-width apart.
  2. Raise your heels off the ground by pushing through the balls of your feet.
  3. Lower your heels back down.

Form Tips:

  • Keep your core engaged for balance.
  • Perform the exercise in a slow and controlled manner.

5. Leg Extensions

Muscles Targeted: Quadriceps

Steps:

  1. Sit at the leg extension machine with your back against the pad.
  2. Extend your legs until they are straight.
  3. Lower the weight back down with control.

Form Tips:

  • Adjust the machine to align your knees with the pivot point.
  • Avoid locking your knees at the top of the movement.

6. Hamstring Curls

Muscles Targeted: Hamstrings

Steps:

  1. Lie face down on the hamstring curl machine.
  2. Secure your ankles under the pad.
  3. Curl your legs towards your glutes.

Form Tips:

  • Keep your hips flat on the bench.
  • Control the movement throughout.

7. Box Jumps

Muscles Targeted: Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Glutes

Steps:

  1. Stand in front of a sturdy box or platform.
  2. Bend your knees and jump onto the box, landing softly.
  3. Step back down and repeat.

Form Tips:

  • Choose a box height suitable for your fitness level.
  • Land with your knees slightly bent to absorb the impact.
