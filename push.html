1. Barbell Bench Press

Muscles Targeted: Chest, shoulders, triceps

Steps:

  1. Lie on a flat bench with your feet flat on the floor.
  2. Grip the barbell with hands slightly wider than shoulder-width.
  3. Lower the bar to your chest, then press it back up to the starting position.

Form Tips:

  • Keep your back flat on the bench.
  • Elbows should be at a 90-degree angle at the bottom.
  • Use a spotter for safety.

2. Dumbbell Shoulder Press

Muscles Targeted: Shoulders, triceps

Steps:

  1. Sit or stand with a dumbbell in each hand at shoulder height.
  2. Press the dumbbells overhead, fully extending your arms.
  3. Lower the dumbbells back to shoulder height.

Form Tips:

  • Keep your core engaged.
  • Avoid arching your back.
  • Slow Reps.

3. Incline Dumbbell Flyes

Muscles Targeted: Upper chest, shoulders

Steps:

  1. Lie on an incline bench with a dumbbell in each hand.
  2. Start with arms extended and palms facing each other.
  3. Lower the dumbbells out to the sides, then bring them back up.

Form Tips:

  • Maintain a slight bend in your elbows.
  • Control the movement and focus on the chest.

4. Tricep Dips

Muscles Targeted: Triceps, chest

Steps:

  1. Use parallel bars or sturdy surfaces.
  2. Lower your body by bending your elbows until they are at a 90-degree angle.
  3. Push back up to the starting position.

Form Tips:

  • Keep your body upright.
  • Avoid letting your shoulders hunch.

5. Overhead Tricep Extension (with dumbbell or barbell)

Muscles Targeted: Triceps

Steps:

  1. Hold a dumbbell or barbell overhead with both hands.
  2. Lower the weight behind your head by bending your elbows.
  3. Extend your arms to return to the starting position.

Form Tips:

  • Keep your upper arms close to your head.
  • Control the movement.

6. Dumbbell Lateral Raises

Muscles Targeted: Lateral deltoids (shoulders)

Steps:

  1. Stand with a dumbbell in each hand at your sides.
  2. Lift the dumbbells out to the sides until they reach shoulder height.
  3. Lower the dumbbells back down.

Form Tips:

  • Maintain a slight bend in your elbows.
  • Control the movement to avoid swinging.

7. Close-Grip Bench Press

Muscles Targeted: Triceps, inner chest

Steps:

  1. Grip the barbell with hands closer than shoulder-width apart.
  2. Lower the bar to your chest, then press it back up.

Form Tips:

  • Keep your elbows close to your body.
  • Focus on the triceps during the movement.

8. Front Plate Raise

Muscles Targeted: Front deltoids (shoulders)

Steps:

  1. Hold a weight plate with both hands in front of your thighs.
  2. Lift the plate straight up to shoulder height.
  3. Lower the plate back down.

Form Tips:

  • Keep your arms straight but with a slight bend in the elbows.
  • Control the movement.

9. Push-Ups

Muscles Targeted: Chest, shoulders, triceps

Steps:

  1. Start in a plank position with hands slightly wider than shoulder-width.
  2. Lower your chest toward the floor by bending your elbows.
  3. Push back up to the starting position.

Form Tips:

  • Keep your body in a straight line.
  • Engage your core throughout.

10. Tricep Pushdowns (with cable machine)

Muscles Targeted: Triceps

Steps:

  1. Attach a straight bar to a cable machine at chest height.
  2. Stand facing the machine, grab the bar, and push it down.
  3. Fully extend your arms, then return to the starting position.

Form Tips:

  • Keep your elbows close to your body.
  • Control the movement and avoid using momentum.
