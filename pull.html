1. Pull-Ups

Muscles Targeted: Latissimus dorsi, biceps, upper back.

Steps:

  1. Hang from a pull-up bar with an overhand grip.
  2. Pull your body up until your chin is above the bar.
  3. Lower yourself back down with control.

Form Tips:

  • Ensure a full range of motion by lowering yourself completely.
  • Engage your core muscles throughout the exercise.

2. Bent Over Barbell Rows

Muscles Targeted: Lats, rhomboids, lower back.

Steps:

  1. Bend at the hips, keeping your back straight.
  2. Pull the barbell to your lower chest.
  3. Lower the barbell back down.

Form Tips:

  • Maintain a flat back to avoid unnecessary strain.
  • Keep your elbows close to your body during the movement.

3. Lat Pulldowns

Muscles Targeted: Latissimus dorsi, biceps.

Steps:

  1. Sit at a lat pulldown machine with a wide grip.
  2. Pull the bar down to your chest.
  3. Slowly release it back up.

Form Tips:

  • Control the weight to prevent swinging.
  • Keep your chest up and shoulders back throughout.

4. Single-Arm Dumbbell Rows

Muscles Targeted: Lats, rhomboids, lower back.

Steps:

  1. Place one knee and hand on a bench, opposite foot on the floor.
  2. Pull the dumbbell to your hip.
  3. Lower it back down.

Form Tips:

  • Keep your back parallel to the ground for proper isolation.
  • Squeeze your shoulder blades together at the top of the movement.

5. Face Pulls

Muscles Targeted: Rear delts, upper traps, rhomboids.

Steps:

  1. Attach a rope to a cable machine at head height.
  2. Pull the rope towards your face.
  3. Control the release.

Form Tips:

  • Keep your neck in a neutral position to avoid strain.
  • Focus on squeezing your rear delts during the movement.

6. Hammer Curls

Muscles Targeted: Brachialis, brachioradialis, biceps.

Steps:

  1. Hold dumbbells with a neutral grip.
  2. Curl the weights towards your shoulders.
  3. Lower them back down.

Form Tips:

  • Keep your elbows close to your body throughout the movement.
  • Control the descent to maximize muscle engagement.

7. Seated Cable Rows

Muscles Targeted: Lats, rhomboids, biceps.

Steps:

  1. Sit at a cable row machine.
  2. Pull the handles towards your torso.
  3. Extend your arms forward.

Form Tips:

  • Maintain an upright posture throughout the exercise.
  • Squeeze your shoulder blades together at the end of the movement.

8. Chin-Ups

Muscles Targeted: Biceps, upper back, rear delts.

Steps:

  1. Hang from a pull-up bar with an underhand grip.
  2. Pull your body up until your chin is above the bar.
  3. Lower yourself back down.

Form Tips:

  • Engage your lats by pulling your elbows down and back.
  • Control the descent to fully work your muscles.

9. Inverted Rows

Muscles Targeted: Upper back, rhomboids.

Steps:

  1. Set up a bar at hip height.
  2. Hang underneath it and pull your chest up.
  3. Lower your body back down.

Form Tips:

  • Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels.
  • Squeeze your shoulder blades together at the top of the movement.

10. Barbell Shrugs

Muscles Targeted: Trapezius, upper back.

Steps:

  1. Hold a barbell in front of you with an overhand grip.
  2. Shrug your shoulders towards your ears.
  3. Lower them back down.

Form Tips:

  • Keep your arms straight and focus on elevating your shoulders.
  • Avoid rolling your shoulders forward during the shrug.
